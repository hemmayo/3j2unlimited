UI/UX Designer

We invite you to the 3J2Unlimited

join our team!

We love design and useful applications. We are looking for a person who likes challenges and loves all the same things we do. A person who values personal development and shows harmony in leading outstanding projects for awesome people from Silicon Valley and multinational organisations such as FOX Networks.

Your tasks:

  • Design UI, Websites and Mobile Apps
  • Prepare low and high fidelity mockups
  • Prepare UI Guidelines with functionality description
  • Propose innovative solutions for simple problems
  • Prepare IA and flow

We will give you:

  • Implementation of projects for foreign clients (and Silicon Valley occupants)
  • Attractive salary
  • Free English lessons with native speaker
  • Private healthcare and sport package
  • Employment contract or B2B
  • Extensive experience, continuous development and an atmosphere conducive to improving qualifications
  • Full support of the entire team while performing tasks
  • Work using the latest technologies and trends
  • In-house academies broadening your professional knowledge
  • Cold beverages, vulgar Tuesdays and regular trainings of CS, CoH and FIFA
  • Unique office in the centre of Kraków - Main Square

Our requirements:

  • At least 3 years of relevant work experience
  • Experience in designing eCommerce websites
  • Experience in designing Responsive Web Design and HCI
  • Master level knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite
  • Very good knowledge of design tools like Sketch App, Invision, UXpin or similar
  • Very good knowledge of English
  • Very good work organization
  • The desire to share knowledge and tolerance to Andżelika’s constructive criticism
  • Knowledge of limitations and capabilities of HTML5, CSS3 and JS
  • Respect for the team, love for design, regard for the code and full engagement

Nice of you to have:

  • Artistic abilities
  • Experience in mobile application design
  • Experience in designing templates for PrestaShop, Shopify or WordPress
  • Potential for a Team Leadership
