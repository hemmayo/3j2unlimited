About Renshee

Prospects pitched by sales representatives

Renshee is where salespeople pay for access to their targets. Renshee fixes sales by allowing prospects to define the times when they are available to receive pitches by sales representatives and the price their time will cost the representatives.

Eoin was searching for a talented team of designers who fully understood his startup idea and would cover the complete design process for his project, from wireframing, UX design, and responsive web design to full design guidelines and collaboration with the development team during implementation.
