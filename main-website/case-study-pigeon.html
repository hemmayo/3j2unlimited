About Pigeon Studio

We get things movin'

Pigeon Studio is  constantly searching for stories worth telling. They  assemble the  best talent under one roof to do what they are known for - animation.

They  strongly believe that working on  a  project and designing a character is like meeting another person — at first there’s the impression, then the revelation of little details: the way they move, their attitudes and expressions, etc., until finally you perceive the world through the characters’ eyes.
